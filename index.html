Briefing

I build and maintain a wide range of Roblox systems, from combat frameworks and game modes to UI, progression, networking, and backend functionality. I focus on creating responsive, reliable, and scalable solutions that enhance gameplay and player experience. My development process is highly iterative, relying on extensive testing and real gameplay observations to ensure quality and performance.

My primary expertise lies in gameplay and systems development, with vehicle-specific mechanics being an area I have less experience in compared to other disciplines.
